Form a group of 3 students (discussed in class), find a team name. Meet with your team to discuss:
- Think of a topic that has potential societal impact, that is clearly important and interesting to students as the target audience, and that you would be excited to explore more. Your work from class should help here.
- Look online for a dataset that can help you answer the questions. The Instructions on the Project has links to some datasets. 
- Prepare a presentation (slides) to explain your chosen topic, **1 research question** and your **dataset**. The deck will NOT be presented in class and needs to be self-explanatory. See next instructions on what to submit.

## Submitting the Assignment
**WHAT** - To complete the assignment you should create 5-6 slides :
1. Slide 1 - Make a title slide
   1. Add your team name and the full name of all team members
   2. Add a short catchy headline to your topic
2. Slide 2
   1. Tell us what the topic is about and describe why it is important.

Example: Sugar Consumption.  
Sugar is a main ingredient considered in healthy eating which is of high interest to students who want to plan for a healthy life.   

3. On Slide 3 you will write your research question (add two if you cannot decide):
  1. Write a research question related to the topic. Make sure that the question you propose cannot be solved with a simple database query but that it is exploratory in nature. An exploratory question could address identifying the role a certain factor has, comparing factors, predicting what will happen, or characterizing a phenomenon.

  Example: How does sugar in school meals relate to recommended amounts per day at the different universities in my region.


4. On Slide 4 mention the dataset(s) you have found (origin, dimensions, size). Is it enough to answer your question(s)?

**WHERE** - Submit a pdf version of your slide deck here on ecampus
